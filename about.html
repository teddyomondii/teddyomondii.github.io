About us 

 

Situated on the water edge beside the Old Golf Course, Amici reflects the passionate, humble and sociable attitude of Italians towards food. Using only the best local ingredients to produce a rustic, homely and authentic Italian menu. We use a charcoal oven to cook all of our meat, fish and poultry, giving them a subtle smokey flavour.
